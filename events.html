Information on upcoming competition at Red Beard Barbell Club, Maidenhead on 19th March:

On the 26th Feb 1300 - 1500 we are running a beginners comp workshop at the gym to go over everything you’ll need to know on the day to be able to tackle your first competition with a little more confidence, including warm up area, holding area, the board, commands during a lift, what constitutes a good lift etc. plus form checks should you want as well! This is just £5 a head, message me via the gyms instagram if you’d like to book on https://www.instagram.com/redbeardbarbellclub/

On to the comp!

First thing’s first, DO NOT USE THE GYM’S POSTCODE to get here, it takes you to the M4!! Instead if you use Google maps or Waze, type in Red Beard Barbell Club and it’ll bring you right to us. For any other Sat Nav, use Moor Farm, Holyport. When you get to Moor Farm, drive straight in and past the small cottages. There’s some dead cars on the right that belong to a mechanic on site, the gym is in the big barn behind these cars! Park up where you can but please be mindful that it is a working yard and full access is needed at all times around for both business’s on site and the equestrian facility to bring horses in and out of fields etc. So be aware of any gates or access you may be blocking, there is plenty of room to park on site and we will aim to have some directional signage up for the day.

Secondly, there will be 2 weigh ins, first weigh in for Flight 1&2 is at 0800 aiming to close at 0900 with the second weigh in for Flight 3&4 at 1300 aiming to close at 1400.

The gym is quite small so I ask that once the presentations for Flight 1&2 are finished that both flights lifters and spectators leave to make room for the next competition, unless obviously you are handling or supporting lifters in the later flights.

There will be awards on the day for Best Male and Female lifter as well as 1st, 2nd & 3rd trophies for weight classes!

Last of all, please feel free to contact me should you have any questions on getting here on the day on 07854 426 368.
